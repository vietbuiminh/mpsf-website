Supporting students in the Moline-Coal Valley School District

Who we are

The Moline Public Schools Foundation is a fundraising partner of the Moline-Coal Valley School District. Led by an Executive Director and a volunteer board, our work funds projects that empower teachers and bring innovative learning opportunities to students across the district.

What we do

The Moline Public Schools Foundation grant program awards more than $20,000 annually to teachers. Funds are used for creative projects that directly impact student learning.

Why it matters

Quality educational opportunities benefit our children and attract families, businesses, and investors to the area. In partnership with the school district, the MPSF provides teachers with additional resources to fund special projects and connects students in new, exciting ways that fuel a passion for learning.
